Our Story

Bhoomi Real Estate was founded in 2020 by a visionary team of entrepreneurs with extensive experience in both technology and real estate. Their shared goal was to address the challenges and pain points that had long plagued the real estate industry, such as transparency, accessibility, and ease of transactions. The founders aimed to create a platform that could not only streamline property dealings but also empower buyers and sellers with data-driven insights to make informed decisions.

Our Mission

Since its inception, Bhoomi Real Estate has expanded rapidly, not only in terms of its geographical footprint but also in the range of services it offers.
    1.National and International Reach: Bhoomi has made a significant presence in key real estate markets globally. Whether you're looking for a condominium in New York City or a villa in Bali, Bhoomi can help you find the perfect property.
    2.Investment Services: Recognizing the growing interest in real estate as an investment, Bhoomi has introduced investment advisory services, helping individuals build wealth through real estate portfolios.
    3.Property Management: The company also offers property management services, catering to both homeowners and investors. This end-to-end service includes property maintenance, tenant management, and financial reporting.

Our Team

Our team is made up of dedicated professionals who bring expertise and a passion for real estate. We are your partners in this journey, ready to provide guidance, insights, and solutions. Together, we ensure that your real estate experience is not only efficient but also enjoyable.
